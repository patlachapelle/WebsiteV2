Rover Drilling Proposal (With CAD)

Mechanical Engineering

Proposal Written for 2017-18 (Fall/Winter) NASA NCAS Internship

This paper was written for the primary portion of the NASA NCAS internship. This paper was the basis for acceptance into stage 2 of the NCAS internship (which I was selected for) and served as my proposal for NASA's Mars 2020 Rover. I designed the rover primarily for functionality in Experimental scientific applications, including Geologic sampling and Biological analysis for the feasibility of past or present life. The proposal also includes justification for all aspects included in the design.
