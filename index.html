Welcome to my Website

 

An updated version of patlachapelle.us

 

Welcome V2 of my personal website, patlachapelle.us. I orginally created my personal website with the intent of it being a place where I could send professional contacts, professors, advisors, potential employers or anyone else who might be interested in learning about my qualification and experience.

Prior to embarking upon the original endeavor, I viewed websites and HTML to be practically magic. I typed what I wanted to find into the Google Search bar and Google somehow pulled what I wanted out of the universal ether. My original website was, unsurprisingly, awful. Almost a year after building that first website, I decided to apply an additional year's worth of experience and knowledge to building an updated, cleaner, and easier-to-navigate version of my website, utilizing the Bootstrap framework.

 

Thanks for visiting!
