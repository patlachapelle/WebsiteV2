CME Propagation Analysis Poster

Heliophysics, Astronomy

The presentation poster prepared for and presented at the 2018 NASA GSFC Intern Poster Symposium

This poster was created to present research I conducted with fellow intern Ryan Skoletsky, analyzing the performance of the WSA-ENLIL+Cone model during the period of STEREO (Solar TEerrestrial RElations Observatory) quadrature. The WSA-ENLIL+Cone model is the standard model for predicting the arrival of observed Coronal Mass Ejections at Earth. STEREO quadrature refers to the period of time in which the two space craft (STEREO A & STEREO B), were aligned at 90° with respect to Earth's axis with the Sun.
